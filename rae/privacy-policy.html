Privacy Policy

None of your information is collected; however, your data is temporarily stored. Over time, all stored information is deprecated and deleted. Users may opt-out via the application, or contact the developer of the app directly via the public support server. If a user wishes to request/delete their data, they may contact the developer directly via the public support server. The chat bridge feature of the Rae Discord application shares your message data with other servers/users you may not be familiar with. Users may opt-out of this feature. Users, by default, are not opted into this experimental feature.
