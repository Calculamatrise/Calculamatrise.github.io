Rae is a multipurpose bot mainly used for music and moderation. Rae has various features including global chat (cross-server communication), auto role and self roles. There are also certain hidden features such as; temp-no-mic. If you name one of your channels with that name, any message sent to that channel will be deleted within a minute of it being sent.

Command Description
afk Set an afk status. (displays when someone tries to ping you)
anagramatize Anagramatize a word of your choice!
avatar Get someone's avatar.. I guess
back Goes back a song. If you're at the end of the queue, it'll start playing the last song.
clear Purge command; bulk delete messages.
confess Submit a confession.
deafen Timed deafen.
guild Configure guild settings and automation.
guildinfo Get data from any guild via ID.
hug Hug another member.
invite Invite me to your server!
kiss Kiss another member.
mute Timed mute.
pat Pat another member.
pause Pause the moosic...
play Play moosic from youtube.
poke Poke another member.
queue Tamper with the song queue.
rail oop- MATURE CONTENT
resume Resume the currently paused track.
skip Skip the song that is currently playing.
slap Slap another member.
snipe Snipe a recently edited or deleted message.
spam Spam another member.
spank Spank another member. MATURE CONTENT
stop Stop the moosic and leave the voice channel.
undeafen Undeafen a deafened user.
unmute Unmute a muted user.
userinfo Gather data on a discord user.
